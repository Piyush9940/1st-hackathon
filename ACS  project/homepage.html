About Us

A group of college students collaborated to develop a task manager app, "Orgoclub," to address their challenges in managing assignments, extracurriculars, and personal responsibilities. Led by Piyush, the team outlined essential features like task categorization, reminders, and collaboration tools. Piyush handled backend development, Rakshitha designed an intuitive interface.
